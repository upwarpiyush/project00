Vishwakarma Inn in Pune provides adults-only accommodation with a garden and a terrace. The property is located 7 km from Cañaveralejo's Bullring, 9 km from Pan-American Park and 12 km from Jorge Isaacs Theater. The accommodation provides a 24-hour front desk, room service and currency exchange for guests.

The hotel will provide guests with air-conditioned rooms offering a wardrobe, a safety deposit box, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with a shower. Free WiFi is available to all guests, while selected rooms are fitted with a patio. At Vishwakarma Inn each room includes bed linen and towels.

Guests at the accommodation can enjoy a continental or a buffet breakfast.

At Vishwakarma Inn guests are welcome to take advantage of a sauna.

Shaniwarwada is 12 km from the hotel, while Sarasbaug is 12 km away. The nearest airport is Pune Airport, 34 km from Vishwakarma Inn.

Couples particularly like the location — they rated it 9.3 for a two-person trip.
